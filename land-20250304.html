The Checkpoint Session:

What is it? A one-hour remote coaching call that helps you kick-start the process of challenging the frame of what's holding you back. Whether you're wrestling with a career decision, a business challenge, or a personal transition, we’ll explore what types of things are acting against you. We'll then create a clarity checklist together, so you come away with a set of methods and actions to get you started on your transformation.

What do I get out of it?

  • Expert-led clarity. Get an outside perspective from seasoned strategists.
  • A problem reframed. See your challenge from new angles.
  • An immediate action plan. Walk away with clear next steps.

Why do this?

You don’t need another round of overthinking. You need clarity, and that starts right here.

The Breakthrough Workshop:

What is it? A deeper-dive, high-impact strategy session (2 hours, remote, with a preparatory meeting and homework) designed to unlock major breakthroughs.

We'll align our choice of methods to your specific area, using proven strategy techniques to uncover root causes and challenge assumptions. Including the pre-work, the session is long enough to craft a clear outline of your end-to-end change journey. We'll create a map of your problem space and prioritise it based on how much agency you have in the face of each challenge. We'll structure and capture your future vision and we'll work out a plan of how to get you there. You will come out of your breakthrough workshop with a list of actionable insights and clear next steps.

What do I get out of it?

  • End-to-end problem-space clarity. We map out your situation, constraints and opportunities.
  • Bespoke strategy frameworks. We'll use tools specifically tailored to your unique challenge.
  • Your vision and roadmap. A structured assessment of your challenges and your plan to move forward.

Why do this?

If you’ve been stuck for months – or even years – this is how you break through.
